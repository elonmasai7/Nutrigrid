Problem

Malnutrition is a last-mile failure.

Undernutrition, hidden hunger, and food access inequality persist because detection, counseling, and supply chains operate in silos.

Undernutrition

Children are screened too late, after wasting or stunting has already taken hold.

Root causes

  • Infrequent screenings due to distance and workload.
  • Limited referral pathways when risk is detected.
  • Insufficient data feedback loops to program leads.

Hidden hunger

Micronutrient deficiencies persist even when calories are available.

Root causes

  • Low diet diversity and limited animal-source foods.
  • Irregular access to supplements and fortified staples.
  • Inconsistent caregiver counseling.

Food access inequality

Remote households face the highest prices and the weakest supply reliability.

Root causes

  • Fragmented last-mile logistics and stockouts.
  • Poor visibility into inventory across sites.
  • Infrastructure gaps: transport, power, connectivity.

We can fix the loop.

Nutrigrid connects screening, counseling, and supply in one system.
