Solution

One workflow, four decisive steps.

HarvestPulse empowers community health workers with an offline-first toolkit that keeps care and supply aligned.

Step 1: Screen

Capture MUAC, weight, symptoms, and feeding data in under 2 minutes.

Offline checklist

Works with or without connectivity. Sync when signal is available.

Risk scoring

Lightweight model flags moderate and severe risk early.

Step 2: Counsel

Culturally relevant counseling scripts help caregivers act immediately.

Examples

  • Local food swaps for protein and iron.
  • Feeding frequency reminders.
  • Seasonal preparation tips.

Step 3: Supply

Dispense supplements and log inventory with a single tap.

Inventory logic

  • Track MNP, RUTF, and fortified staples.
  • Auto-calculate buffer stock thresholds.
  • Flag urgent resupply needs.

Step 4: Predict

Predictive resupply keeps district hubs ahead of demand.

Forecast signals

  • Monthly screening volume.
  • Risk distribution changes.
  • Consumption velocity by site.
